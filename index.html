01 / What it is

A single, quiet home for every part of the game.

Pickleball grew faster than the software around it. Group chats, spreadsheets, scribbled brackets, three different apps for one league. PickleCue is the app that finally ties it all together.

We built PickleCue for players, organizers, and clubs who want one clean place to find a court, fill a game, run a season, and remember who won.

Every feature is designed to feel like a native part of iOS — fast, private, considered. No ads. No data sold. No noise.

Pre-launch access is free. Join the waitlist and we’ll let you know the day it’s live on the App Store.

04 / Questions

Anything worth knowing before launch.

When does PickleCue launch?

Soon. We’re finishing up the final pre-submission pass and preparing for App Store review. Waitlist members get the launch email the moment it’s live — before we announce anywhere else.

Is it free?

The core app is free — finding courts, joining games, logging matches, following friends. A small Pro subscription unlocks organizer-grade features like unlimited leagues, advanced tournament formats, and CSV / JSON match exports. No ads at any tier.

What platforms?

iOS first, built natively for iPhone. Android and a web app are on the roadmap. We’d rather do one platform extremely well than three platforms adequately.

Do I need a DUPR account?

No. You can use PickleCue entirely without one. If you do have a DUPR account, you can optionally link it to push your match results through for rating updates — or keep your matches private. Your call, toggle any time.

What do you do with my data?

As little as possible. The waitlist form collects only your email and a source tag. We don’t sell data, we don’t share it with advertisers, and we never will. Full detail in the privacy policy.

I organize a club / league / tournament — can I help shape this?

Please do. Email feedback@picklecue.com with what you run, what’s painful about it today, and we’ll get you into an early organizer cohort before launch.
