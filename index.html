Pickleball Tips & Resources

Level up your game with expert advice

Strategy

Master the Third Shot Drop

The third shot drop is essential for transitioning from baseline to kitchen. Practice soft, arcing shots that land in the non-volley zone.

Fitness

Quick Court Footwork Drills

Good footwork wins points. Try ladder drills and lateral shuffles to improve your court coverage and reaction time.

Equipment

Choosing the Right Paddle Weight

Lighter paddles (7-7.8 oz) offer control, while heavier paddles (8.5+ oz) provide power. Find your sweet spot based on your play style.
