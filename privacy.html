Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 27, 2026

PickleCue ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, display name, username, and profile photo or emoji avatar
  • Profile Data: Skill level, bio, play preferences, and home city you choose to share
  • Game Data: Games you create, join, or participate in, including RSVPs and waitlists
  • Match History: Scores, match results, and performance statistics you log
  • Communications: Messages sent through in-app chat, including direct messages and league chat
  • Social Connections: Friends you add and interact with
  • Court Reviews: Ratings, reviews, and comments you submit for pickleball courts
  • Reports: Information you provide when reporting other users or content

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type and operating system version
  • Location Data: With your permission, we collect precise location (latitude and longitude) to show nearby courts, games, leagues, and weather conditions
  • Usage Data: App interactions, features used, search queries, and session duration
  • Crash and Performance Data: Error logs, crash reports, and performance metrics to improve stability

Optional Integrations

  • HealthKit: If you choose to connect HealthKit, we can read calories, heart rate, steps, and workout data, and write pickleball workout summaries. HealthKit data is processed entirely on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.
  • DUPR: If you choose to link your DUPR account, we facilitate match result exchanges with DUPR for rating calculations. See "Third-Party Services" below.

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the PickleCue service
  • Match you with nearby players, courts, and games
  • Enable messaging between players
  • Calculate and display match statistics
  • Display weather conditions at courts
  • Process and manage your subscription
  • Send notifications about games and messages
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve the app (only with your consent via App Tracking Transparency)
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and objectionable content
  • Comply with legal obligations

Third-Party Services

We use the following services to operate PickleCue:

  • Supabase: Backend database and authentication. Stores user accounts, profiles, games, matches, messaging, and subscription data. Encrypted in transit and at rest with row-level security policies.
  • Mixpanel: Analytics platform. Receives anonymized usage data linked only to your UUID. No email, name, or personally identifiable information is sent. Only active if you authorize tracking via App Tracking Transparency.
  • Sentry: Crash reporting. Receives anonymized error data linked only to your UUID. No email, name, or personally identifiable information is sent.
  • DUPR: If you link your DUPR account, we share match results (player DUPR IDs and scores) for rating updates. Tokens stored server-side only. You can unlink anytime.
  • Apple WeatherKit: Location coordinates sent for court weather conditions. No user identity included.
  • Apple Push Notification Service: Device token stored for push notifications. Disable anytime in iOS Settings.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT sell your personal data to third parties
  • We do NOT use your data for advertising purposes
  • We do NOT track you across other apps or websites
  • We do NOT share your location with advertisers
  • We do NOT transmit HealthKit data off your device

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, which provides:

  • Encrypted data transmission (TLS 1.3)
  • Encrypted data at rest (AES-256)
  • Row-level security policies
  • SOC 2 Type II compliance

Authentication is handled through secure tokens. We never store your password directly in the app.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Other Users: Your profile, match history, and reputation are visible to other players based on your privacy settings
  • Service Providers: The third-party services listed above
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfers: If we merge with or are acquired by another company

Your Privacy Controls

In the app's Settings > Privacy, you can:

  • Set Profile Visibility: Choose between Everyone, Friends Only, or Private
  • Control who can see your location (exact, city-level, or hidden)
  • Hide your activity status
  • Hide your match history
  • Control search discoverability
  • Block messages from non-friends
  • Block and mute other users

When you enable Private mode, other users see a "This Profile is Private" notice. Your display name and avatar remain visible for identification in games.

Location Data

We collect location data only when:

  • You grant permission in iOS Settings
  • The app is actively in use (not in background)

You can revoke location permission at any time in iOS Settings.

Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your data while your account is active. You can delete your account at any time through the app (Settings > Delete Account) or by emailing privacy@picklecue.com.

When you delete your account:

  • Your profile is immediately removed
  • Your match history is anonymized
  • Your messages are deleted
  • Your push notification tokens are deactivated
  • Your data is purged from our systems within 30 days

Analytics data (Mixpanel) is retained for up to 12 months. Crash data (Sentry) is retained for up to 90 days.

Children's Privacy

PickleCue is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data
  • Export your data (Pro subscribers can export in CSV or JSON format)
  • Opt out of analytics (deny App Tracking Transparency, or change in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking)

We do not sell personal information. California residents have additional rights under CCPA. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes through the app or via email.

Contact Us

For privacy questions or concerns:

  • Privacy: privacy@picklecue.com
  • Support: support@picklecue.com

This privacy policy is effective as of March 27, 2026.
